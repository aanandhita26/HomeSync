# HomeSync 🏠

### Making household responsibilities visible, shared, and fair.

HomeSync started from a simple observation: in many homes, one person ends up managing everything—not just doing chores, but remembering them, planning them, and reminding everyone else about them.

The problem isn't the work itself. It's that most of the effort is invisible.

HomeSync is a household coordination platform that helps families track responsibilities, distribute tasks, and recognize contributions. Instead of relying on one person to mentally manage the home, the system makes responsibilities visible and shared among everyone.

---

## What it does

* Breaks large chores into smaller actionable tasks
* Assigns responsibilities to household members
* Tracks completion and participation
* Uses points and leaderboards to encourage involvement
* Automates recurring household tasks

---

## Why I built it

Growing up, I noticed that household work often goes unnoticed—not because people don't help, but because one person usually carries the responsibility of remembering everything.

HomeSync is my attempt to solve that problem.

The goal isn't to help people do more chores.

The goal is to make effort visible, encourage shared responsibility, and reduce the mental burden placed on a single person.
